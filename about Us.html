About Us

Hydri Enterprises started in 1996,situated in the hardware market in Chennai. Our products are an ideal combination of durable & strength resistance and are guaranteed by the strict quality checks applied during its process.

We are the giant stockist of TVS comprising allen bolts, allen countersunk head screws, hex head bolts and hex nuts. We also deal in other brands of Allen bolts, Hex head bolts, Hex nuts, Socket Head cap screws, Grub screws Nylock nut and Left hand Nuts.

We supply material in States like Pondicherry and TamilNadu, places in tamilnadu like like Erode, Hosur and vadalur and localy in Chennai
